您是否注意到…？

注意：本练习可以由学生单独或两人一组完成，也可以全班一起完成。

看一下图中的两个网络。直观比较和对比这两个网络。记下用于每个网络设计的设备。因为设备已经做了标记，所以您已经知道每个网络上终端设备和中间设备的类型。

这两个网络有何不同？只是网络 B 比网络 A 有更多设备吗？

假设您拥有一家中小型企业，请选择您将会使用的网络。能够根据成本、速度、端口、可扩展性和可管理性来阐释您选择某一网络的理由。

The figure shows two networks. Network A consists of a PC connected to router six, which is connected to router seven. Router seven is connected to a server. Network B consists of a PC connected to a switch. The switch is connected to router three. Router three is connected to Router four with a serial connection. Router three is also connected to router five with an Ethernet connection. Router five is also connected to router four with an Ethernet connection. Router four is connected to a server, and router five is connected to a wireless router that has a tablet connected to it.

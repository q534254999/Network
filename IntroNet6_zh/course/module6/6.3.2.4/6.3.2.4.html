show version 输出

如图所示，show version 命令会显示路由器当前所运行的 Cisco IOS 软件的版本信息、引导程序版本信息以及硬件配置信息（包括系统存储器大小）。

Figure shows sample output from the “show version” command. Highlighted information includes: The IOS version; ROM version; image file location; amount of RAM; number and type of interfaces; and the amount of NVRAM and Flash used.
